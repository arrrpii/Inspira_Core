Mathematics for CS

Instructor: Anna Markosyan

4.4 (1,134 reviews) Intermediate level 6 months 31 enrolled
  • Discrete Mathematics: Master set theory, logic, relations, and functions as the foundation of computer science.
  • Graph Theory: Learn about nodes, edges, paths, and algorithms like Dijkstra's for solving graph-related problems.
  • Combinatorics and Probability: Understand permutations, combinations, and probability concepts applied in algorithms and cryptography.
  • Linear Algebra Basics: Explore matrices, vectors, and transformations relevant in machine learning and graphics.
  • Algorithms and Complexity: Learn mathematical tools to analyze algorithms for efficiency and correctness.
  • Level : Intermediate (requires basic math knowledge)
  • Duration : 8 weeks (3-5 hours per week)
  • Format : Online, self-placed with recorded lectures and interactive assignments
  • Prerequisites : Familiarity with high-school algebra and basic programming concepts

Who Should Take This Course?

  • Aspiring data scientists or AI/ML enthusiasts.
